About

The TKN Game (short for Tao Kae Noi) is an engaging tycoon-style game available on the Roblox platform. Players immerse themselves in the world of seaweed production, where they can create and manage their own seaweed empires. The game challenges players to harvest raw seaweed from virtual farms, build factories, and sell their products in 7-11 stores. Along the way, players must overcome obstacles like natural disasters, sea creatures, and the costs of building and maintaining their factory operations.

Purpose of the game

The TKN Game is designed to create a unique brand experience for players while fostering a connection with the Tao Kae Noi brand, Asia's leading seaweed snack company. By playing the game, players gain insights into the entire process of seaweed production, from harvesting to selling, while having fun. This interactive experience helps attract younger audiences and builds lasting brand loyalty.

Promotional Opportunities

The TKN Game offers exciting promotional features that combine virtual gameplay with real-world rewards:

Real-World Redemption

Players can collect special in-game items that can be redeemed for real Tao Kae Noi seaweed packages available at 7-11 stores. This feature has been highly successful, with over 8,000 seaweed packages redeemed in less than two months.

Virtual to Real Connection

Players can purchase special in-game items using Robux or by buying Tao Kae Noi seaweed packages, which include redemption codes for virtual goods. This integration creates a seamless bridge between the virtual game and real-world products, encouraging engagement and brand interaction.
